Our Mission

At Deferredly, we’re committed to expanding opportunity for aspiring MBA candidates from all backgrounds. We’re working to break down barriers to business education by building accessible tools, transparent resources, and a supportive community to help level the playing field.

Why Deferred MBA Programs?

Programs like HBS 2+2, Stanford Deferred Enrollment, and Wharton Moelis Advance Access allow undergraduates to secure their MBA spot early, providing certainty and focus for their early career development.
